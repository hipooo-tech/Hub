About My PYQ Hub

My PYQ Hub is a free, student-first platform for finding previous year question papers — for university exams and competitive entrance tests alike.

Search by course, semester, year, or exam type and get instant access to relevant papers. View them in-browser or download for offline use.

Built as an open educational resource. No login required.
